About STACKLY

Innovating Specialty Medicine Delivery. Built on Trust, Driven by Care.

The STACKLY Story

STACKLY was founded by a team of logistics experts and pharmacists who recognized a critical gap in the specialty medication supply chain: reliability. Patients dealing with complex conditions like cancer, MS, or severe cardiac issues need absolute assurance that their temperature-sensitive and high-value drugs will arrive on time and intact.

We built STACKLY to be the most secure link between the pharmacy and the patient, utilizing advanced cold chain technology, real-time monitoring, and dedicated last-mile couriers, ensuring medication integrity is never compromised.

Our Mission

To deliver specialty medications with **unrivaled speed, security, and integrity**, empowering patients and providers with confidence in the pharmaceutical supply chain.

— Reliability in every degree.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for specialty pharmaceutical logistics, driven by technological innovation and compassionate patient-centric service.

— Setting the standard for care.

Milestones in Our Journey

2018: Founding & Seed Funding

STACKLY is officially launched with a focus on oncology meds, securing initial funding to develop proprietary temperature monitoring hardware.

2020: National Cold Chain Certification

Achieved full national certification for cold chain logistics, establishing compliance for biologics and specialty injectables across all 50 states.

2022: Launch of Patient Tracking App

Released the STACKLY mobile app, providing patients with real-time GPS tracking and 24/7 pharmacist consultation access.

2024: Expanded Specialties & Partnerships

Expanded service lines to include Cardiology and Neurology specialties. Secured major partnerships with leading U.S. hospital networks and pharmaceutical manufacturers.

Your fast track to pharmaceutical delivery. Always reliable, always on time.
