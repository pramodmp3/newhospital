STACKLY FAQ

Your questions about specialized medicine delivery, answered with clarity and trust.

Oncology Medicines & Delivery

Q: How do you ensure the temperature control for chemotherapy drugs? ▼

A: We use validated, medical-grade cold chain packaging, including specialized thermal shippers and phase-change materials. All packages are tracked with temperature monitoring sensors to maintain the required cold chain (typically 2-8°C) from our pharmacy to your doorstep. We provide real-time status updates.

Q: What security measures are in place for high-cost or high-risk oncology medications? ▼

A: High-cost medications are handled with an enhanced chain-of-custody protocol. This includes secure, tamper-evident packaging, dedicated delivery personnel, and a mandatory ID check and signature upon receipt by the patient or authorized caregiver, ensuring accountability and security throughout the transit process.

Q: Can I schedule recurring monthly deliveries for maintenance therapy? ▼

A: Yes, STACKLY specializes in scheduled deliveries. We will work with you and your prescriber to set up a consistent monthly or bi-weekly delivery schedule, ensuring you never miss a dose. We'll send automated reminders before each shipment.

Orthopedics Pain Management & Biologics

Q: How do I order specialized injectables like hyaluronic acid or biologics? ▼

A: Your physician can send the prescription directly to STACKLY. We will handle all insurance verification and prior authorization. Once approved, we coordinate with you for a delivery time that ensures the medication arrives safely and is ready for administration.

Q: Are controlled substances for post-operative pain delivered differently? ▼

A: Yes, controlled pain medications follow strict regulatory guidelines. They require explicit verification of the recipient's identity and cannot be left unattended. Our drivers are trained to follow federal and state laws, ensuring a secure, person-to-person handoff.

Neurology Medications & Handling

Q: I need an epilepsy medication immediately. What is your fastest delivery option? ▼

A: For urgent needs, we offer express, same-day delivery service within local zones, pending inventory availability and prescription verification. Please call our 24/7 support line immediately after sending the prescription so we can expedite the process.

Q: Do you offer specialty medications for Multiple Sclerosis (MS) or Parkinson's disease? ▼

A: Absolutely. We stock a comprehensive list of specialty neurology drugs. Our specialty pharmacy team offers patient education, adherence monitoring, and dedicated clinical support for complex therapies.

Cardiology & Chronic Care

Q: Can I manage my ongoing chronic heart medication refills through the STACKLY app? ▼

A: Yes. Our dedicated mobile application allows you to track all prescriptions, request refills with a single tap, set dosage reminders, and manage your delivery address and schedule for all your chronic care medications.

Q: What is STACKLY's policy on medication returns or exchanges? ▼

A: Due to regulatory requirements and to ensure the integrity of our specialized drugs, we generally cannot accept returned medication once it has left our facility, except in cases of dispensing error by our pharmacy. Please contact our support team immediately if there is an issue with your order.
