Our Journey: Healing, Innovating, Leading

Stackly Hospitals is dedicated to integrating cutting-edge technology with compassionate, patient-centered care. Discover how we became a global leader in healthcare innovation.

The Stackly Hospitals Story

1985: A Vision for Integrated Care

Stackly Hospitals was founded in 1985 by Dr. Evelyn Reed with the vision of creating a medical center where technology and human touch coexist seamlessly. Starting as a small research clinic focused on early computerized diagnostics, our mission quickly expanded.

2005: Global Expansion & Digital Transformation

The early 2000s marked a pivotal era. We pioneered the "Stackly Digital Patient Record," making us one of the first hospital systems to fully digitize patient data. This foundation allowed us to scale, opening our first international centers in London and Bangalore.

Today: The Future of Medicine

We continue to lead, integrating Generative AI for diagnostics and advanced robotics for surgery. Our focus remains on delivering the highest quality care while advancing medical science worldwide.
